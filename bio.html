Nike Air Force 1 X Off White

This sneaker is a release from Nike its part of the “The Ten” collection it was a collaboration between Nike and the head of designer label OffWhite Virgil Abloh; in which Nike released ten of their iconic designs. Although the collection is for the Air force 1s are called The Ten Only 9 of the sneakers, are Nike the latter being a pair of Converse All stars (Although Converse is a subsidiary of Nike). The collection within The ten includes; Nike Air Jordan 1’s, Nike Prestos, Nike Air Max 97, Nike Air Vapormax, Nike Air Max 90, Nike Blazer, Nike Air Force 1’s, Nike React Hyperdunks, Nike Zoom Fly and Chuck Taylor Converse Allstars.
For the Air force 1, the design for this sneaker is a refashioning of the original iconic model. It has been deconstructed in regards to the base usually Airforces may be made from leather or suede but this has padding removed also featuring a suede upper, on the side panels the white nike swoosh has a deconstructed aesthetic appearing to be stitched into the upper sole. There is also an orange rectangular patch sewn into the nike swoosh. The lower sole features the labelling “AIR” beside the original engraved AIR within the lower sole, distinctive because within the brand OffWhite that type of design in terms of deconstruction or labelling using apostrophes is a hallmark or trademark in a way of OffWhite it is used on the majority of their products.

Adidas Yeezy 700

The Adidas Yeezy 700 is a sneaker designed by Kayne West one of his many releases in collaboration with Adidas. Kanye previously worked for Nike on the Air Yeezys but that relationship ended un amicably because he wanted royalties for the sneakers he was producing, Adidas managed to offer this to Kanye and so he went on to design his range of sneakers for them.
This particular sneaker features 5 colourways; Solid grey, Mauve, Salt, Inertia and Analog. With the Version 2 model featuring two colours which are; Geode and static. The silhouette that was first released were the solid greys, a range of colours are used on the sneakers design, dark contrasting colours of blue and teal or cyan outer sole, mixed with bolder neon colours such as; blue, green and orange sporadically but sensibly used on the lower sole and on the upper sole and laces. A few materials feature on the 700s like soft nylon, suede and rubber.

Adidas NMD Human Race

Pharrell Williams a famous musician producer and designer produced this sneaker in partnership with Adidas. The first sneaker released was the ‘Yellow’ colour way featuring the words ‘Human Race’ across the laces and front panel. Since then other colourways have been produced from; ‘Black’, ‘Blue’, ‘Green’, ‘Red’, and ‘Tangerine’ even another variant collaboration with Chanel.
This model usually generates allot of hype and demand behind it making the resale value inflate higher than the retail value. For example the first original colour way the Yellow NMD HU’s are valued at a resale price ranging from £1000-3000 depending on size availability on the StockX Marketplace, from an initial retail price of £170. The NMD HU’s are modelled after the original NMD’s released in 2015 whilst the Human race versions were release in 2016. The NMD HU’s feature EVA plugs on the Ultraboost midsole of the sneaker which act as a brace for the laces to run through, with a Primeknit upper.

Nike Air Max 1/97

The Air Max 1/97 are a hybrid of two of Nikes staple models the Air Max 1 and 97’s. Designed by a Mr Sean Wotherspoon who is a Youtuber and the owner of a vintage store by the name of Round-Two; he is also an avid sneaker collector.
The products final aesthetic look came through discussions with friends Sean says in an interview with Nike, his friend even sketched the suggestions that they were developing. He combined the upper design of a Air Max 97 with a midsole and outsole of a Air Max 1, The upper sole of the sneaker is a multicoloured corduroy design, with pastel colours of yellow, brown, pink and blue; making the sneaker very distinctive in combination with the hybrid model. The lower sole is white featuring an Air bubble of an Air Max 90 the ‘infrared’ which has a red inner structure within the Air bubble; the tread at the bottom of the sole is also in the pastel colour.
